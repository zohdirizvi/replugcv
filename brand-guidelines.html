Brand Overview

What ReplugCV is and what it stands for

ReplugCV is an AI-powered resume builder designed for designers, developers, and modern professionals who apply strategically — not spray-and-pray.

Our core innovation is the base/variant system — inspired by Git branching. Create one base resume, then generate tailored variants for every job application. Change once, propagate everywhere.

We exist because the resume industry is broken: bait-and-switch pricing, generic AI content, unverifiable ATS claims, and zero collaboration. ReplugCV is the antidote — transparent, precise, and built by people who understand the problem because they lived it.

One base. Infinite variants.
Primary tagline — used across all touchpoints

Brand Voice

Direct. Technical. Transparent. Witty, not cute.
✓ We say
"Your resume is probably costing you interviews. Let's fix that."
✗ We don't say
"STOP sending bad resumes! You're DESTROYING your career!"
✓ We say
"We built the resume tool we wanted to exist."
✗ We don't say
"We're the only resume builder that actually works."
✓ We say
"Here's exactly how your ATS score is calculated. No black boxes."
✗ We don't say
"Our proprietary NLP engine leverages transformer architectures..."
✓ We say
"Base resume. Infinite variants. Zero existential dread."
✗ We don't say
"Wanna make your resume sparkle? We've got the magic! ✨"

Words we use: variant, base, score, analyze, build, craft, system, transparent, precise

Words we avoid: version, master, rating, scan, create, polish, platform, solution, smart, intelligent
